Adventure Tours & Packages

Mountain Hiking Adventure

Traverse cloud-forest ridges, camp under the stars and reach 3 800 m summit at sunrise. Wildlife spotting included.

  • Duration: 3 days / 2 nights
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Group size: max 10

What's included: Local transport, eco-guide, camping gear, vegetarian meals, conservation fee.

LKR 95,000

Forest Camping Experience

Unplug completely in an off-grid solar camp. Learn fire-making, animal tracking and night-sky navigation.

  • Duration: 2 days / 1 night
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Group size: max 12

What's included: Transfers, tent (shared double), all meals, workshop materials, park permits.

LKR 45,000

River Rafting Expedition

Navigate class III-IV rapids through pristine canyon. Combine adrenaline with riverside ecology talks.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Difficulty: Moderate-Challenging
  • Group size: max 8

What's included: Raft equipment, safety briefing, riverside lunch, waterproof camera photos.

LKR 40,000

Eco Cycling Tour

Half-day guided e-bike ride visiting organic coffee farms and hidden waterfalls with plenty of tasting stops.

  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy (e-bike assisted)
  • Group size: max 12

What's included: E-bike & helmet, local tastings, reusable water bottle, support vehicle.

LKR 30,000
