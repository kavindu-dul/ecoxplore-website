About EcoXplore Adventures

Founded in 2023 by a collective of biologists and outdoor instructors, EcoXplore Adventures exists to prove that responsible travel can be exhilarating. We craft small-group expeditions that adhere to the seven Leave-No-Trace principles and donate 5 % of every booking to grassroots conservation NGOs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Provide life-changing outdoor experiences while actively protecting the ecosystems we visit.

Vision: A tourism industry where travellers, host communities and wildlife all thrive together.
